Our Creative Team

We are a passionate team of four, combining innovation and technology to develop sustainable solutions. With EcoClothBin, we aim to revolutionize textile waste management, making recycling smarter and more efficient. By leveraging our expertise in environmental technologies, software development, and engineering, we strive to create a cleaner and more sustainable future. Every detail matters, and we are committed to making a positive impact on the world.

Project Manager

Ömer Tekin Yavuz

Ömer Tekin Yavuz is responsible for the overall coordination of the project. The system design and project planning are carried out under his leadership. He also monitors the team's progress to ensure they stay on schedule. He is responsible for the final evaluation and project report.

Electrical Engineer

Mehmet Enes Şahin

Mehmet Enes Şahin is responsible for the electrical and hardware integration of the donation box. He integrates the servo motor and lid mechanism into the system, ensuring the donation box operates smoothly.

Windows Application Developer

Muhsin Ay

Muhsin Ay is the developer responsible for creating the Windows Forms-based management panel. Using DevExpress, he designs a user-friendly and functional interface for efficient data handling.

Web Developer

Yunus Emre Karataş

Yunus Emre Karataş plays an active role in both frontend and backend development. He develops a fully functional and responsive web interface using HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. He is also responsible for backend development with Node.js and integrating PostgreSQL for secure data handling.

Vision

At EcoClothBin, our goal is to revolutionize textile waste management and make recycling smarter by leveraging technology. We strive to contribute to a cleaner and greener future, working tirelessly to create a more sustainable world across the globe.

Mission

Our mission is to recycle textile waste efficiently and intelligently. By combining our expertise in engineering and software development, we are committed to creating innovative solutions and making a real difference in sustainability.
